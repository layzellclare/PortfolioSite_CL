My Story so far...

After completing my final design project in the heady world of Architecture school, I cut my creative teeth in a small architectural firm in Holborn, London. It would be fair to say, the move from the unbridled design of Architecture school to the reality of industry somewhat dampened my spirits for a while. Humbled by the advice and achievements of my peers, better times and better designs ensued; many years of collaborating and/or being the lead designer on a variety of retail/mixed use and cultural schemes, extensive travelling, amazing food in amazing locations and continued learning from some of the best and well known people in the business. So, why UX? Truth be known, I was never that interested in the more technical aspects of Architecture; research, conceptual design and the processes that transformed findings into 3D reality were my thing. More and more, I found myself craving a release from the constraints of Architecture. My self imposed change of career has opened my eyes to new processes and ways of working and I bring a myriad of assets to the table - none more important than my unwavering desire to create striking and relevant design. I’m an inquisitive thinker, a confident communicator and a self confessed detail obsessor.
